  • 2008 - Makes a speech at Peshawar Press Club about education rights
  • 2009 - Starts blogging for BBC anonymously about Tiliban in Swat
  • 2011 - Given the Nation Youth Peace Prize
  • 2012 - Shot by the Taliban gunmen, Flies to UK for treatment
  • 2013 - Discharged from Queen Elizabeth Hospital, Nominated for Nobel Peace Prize
